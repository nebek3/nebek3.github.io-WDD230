Target Audience

The audience most interested in this website are those making travel plans or looking for a good day to hold an outdoor activity.

Persona 1

Michael is a college student. He and his friends would like to have a sports day together but the weather hasnt' been very good recently. They need to find out how long Weather is going to ruin their plans.

Persona 2

Jenna is also a college student. However, she'd rather travel around for the weekend to have her fun. Weather has made that difficult with all its recent storms. She'd like to know when things will clear up.

Scenerios

  • It looks like today it's going to snow. Shouldn't play football.
  • This weekend shows clear skies, let's head to the park for spikeball!
  • Will the weather be okay for driving home this week?
  • There's a lot of wind the next few days. Let's do our running inside.
  • What's the weather like next weekend? I may have to leave a day early.
